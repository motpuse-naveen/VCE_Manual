Service position

BEFORE you begin service service work the machine must be placed on level ground and prepared as shown below.

AFTER you have completed completed the service, any guard plates must be re-installed and all engine covers are to be closed and locked.

Lock the articulation joint.

The attachment should be resting on the ground, or the loader arms should be safely supported.

Apply the parking brake. Turn off the engine and leave the ignition key in position 1.

Carefully release the pressure in pressure lines and accumulators to avoid risks.

Attach a black and yellow label to the steering wheel.

Block the wheels in a safe way, e.g. with wheel blocks.

Switch off the battery disconnect switch.

Allow the machine to cool.
